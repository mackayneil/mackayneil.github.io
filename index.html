All about me

“Don’t sacrifice tomorrow’s memories by being economical today” - Someone special

I believe that if you want to do something you should go out and get it done. Don’t let people hold you back. If there is an obstacle in your way find a solution to get over it or get around it. When I was 18 I wanted to travel the world, but I couldn’t afford to hop on a plane and start travelling. So I helped deliver a 39ft catamaran by sailing across the Atlantic Ocean from Cape Town to Florida. This led me to start my career in the superyacht industry which allowed me to see the world. I’ve since left that industry, but the lessons I’ve learned along the way will be with me forever.

“Anyone who stops learning is old, whether at twenty or eighty” - Henry Ford

I'm a self taught UI/UX designer and front-end web developer. I'm an avid student who constantly seeks new learning opportunities. I didn't follow the traditional four year college route, but that doesn’t mean that I stopped learning after high school. I’ve found that there is always something new to learn everyday, no matter how big or small. If I get stuck on something I don’t hesitate to do thorough research for the answer, or enroll in a new online course to further expand my knowledge on a subject.

“I meant what I said and I said what I meant.” - Dr. Seuss

Lying never ends well. I’d much rather tell the truth about something, especially if it has to do with my capabilities of being able to do a task. If I don’t know how to do it I’ll be upfront and let you know. I’ll also let you know that I’ll do my best to figure out how to do the task, but pretending to know everything is not me.

Ecommerce store

An ecommerce website that provides unformation about their products and sells to the public via their website

The project

This project involved creating an entire eccomerce store where users can make purchases online and have the product delivered to them. I built the original site on Wix, but after a year the decision was made to rebuild it on Shopify.

My goal

My goal for this project was to provide enough information to users on what Kombucha is, as it was fairly new to South Africa when the site launched. I also wanted to make it easy for users to navigate through the site, find out where they could purchase the product from and be able to make purchases online easily.

What I did

Created the wireframe for the design layout of the homepage.
Built the front-end of all the other pages using HTML and CSS. This included making sure they were responsive across multiple device sizes.
Set up the backend of the Shopify store which included uploading all products, shipping details, activating payment methods.
Created and edited all the product photos.

Travel blog

An online travel diary for the user to share their travel experiences to their audience.

The project

This project involved designing a blog / travel diary where users can post their experiences and their audience can view them.

My goal

My goal was to make an easy to navigate web page that focuses on the image taken by the traveller. I also wanted to experiment with different page transitions using Invision.

What I did

Researched different travel blogs and designs for inspiration.
Designed the page layouts using Sketch.
Imported the pages into Invision and created a working prototype featuring transitions between the pages.
