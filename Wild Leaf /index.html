Wild Leaf Kombucha

Wild Leaf Kombucha is a Cape Town based Kombucha company that sells their product online as well as a variety of stores.

The project

This project involved creating an entire eccomerce store where users can make purchases online and have the product delivered to them.
I built the original site on Wix, but after a year the decision was made to rebuild it on Shopify.

My goal

My goal for this project was to provide enough information to users on what Kombucha is, as it was fairly new to South Africa when the site launched.
I also wanted to make it easy for users to navigate through the site, find out where they could purchase the product from and be able to make purchases online easily.

What I did

  • Created the wireframe for the design layout of the homepage
  • Built the front-end of all the other pages using HTML and CSS. This included making sure they were responsive across multiple device sizes.
  • Set up the backend of the Shopify store which included uploading all products, shipping details, activating payment methods.
  • Created and edited all the product photos.
